# Prompting is Not an Input. It’s a System.

Most people treat prompting like this:

→ Write a prompt  
→ Get an output  

But in real-world usage, prompting behaves like a system.

---

## Why Prompting Breaks

- No standard structure  
- No versioning  
- No evaluation  
- No reuse  

This leads to inconsistency and unpredictability.

---

## What Changes Everything

When you treat prompting as:

→ Intent  
→ Context  
→ Constraints  
→ Output structure  

You move from guessing → designing.

---

## Prompting in Production

In real products, prompts need:

- consistency  
- measurability  
- iteration loops  

Without this, AI cannot scale.

---

## Takeaway

Prompting is not about better wording.

It’s about better structure.

That’s what makes AI reliable.
